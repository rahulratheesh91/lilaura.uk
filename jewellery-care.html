Jewellery Care FAQs

How should I store my jewellery?

Store your jewellery separately in a soft-lined box or pouch to prevent scratches. Avoid direct sunlight, moisture, and contact between pieces.

Can I wear gold jewellery every day?

Gold jewellery can be worn daily, but we recommend removing it during exercise, cleaning, swimming, or sleeping to preserve its finish.

How do I clean my jewellery safely?

Use a soft cloth and warm water with mild soap. Avoid harsh chemicals, ultrasonic cleaners, or abrasive materials.

Will perfumes or lotions damage my jewellery?

Yes. Apply perfumes, lotions, and cosmetics before wearing jewellery to minimise chemical exposure and maintain shine.

How often should jewellery be professionally cleaned?

We recommend professional inspection and cleaning once a year, especially for fine jewellery with stones.
